<h1>Princeton Poh</h1>

For better or for worse, I'm addicted to the startup game. 


In college, I co-founded [Scratchbac](/blog/9050-scratchbac/), a hyperlocal resource-sharing startup.
We did a 30k Kickstarter raise, raised 500k SGD in seed funding, and grew the team from just me to 12 people.


3-4 years later after the company got onto its feet, I left to complete my masters in UC Berkeley and ZheJiang University. 
Oh, I studied Computer Science and Design (CSD) in Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). 
It's the largest design school setup by MIT and I'm proud to be part of [Tom Magnanti](https://www.sutd.edu.sg/profile/tom-magnanti/)'s vision for the future of engineering.


Now, I'm back running a startup, this time in the math education space called [PaperLand](https://paperland.sg/).
Let's connect if you're passionate about education too!
